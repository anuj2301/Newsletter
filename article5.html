IAF to join 20-nation 'Exercise Pitch Black' in Australia with Su-30MKIs fighter jets

ndia Air Force's Su-30 MKI fighter jets are set to take part in a biennial multinational aerial warfare exercise in Australia starting later this month that will be its largest edition with participation from 20 countries across the globe.India will be part of 'Exercise Pitch Black 24' which will take place from July 12 to August 2.These long-range missions are being supported by IAF's inflight refuellers IL-78 aircraft.On return, IAF will also participate in Ex Udara Shakti 24 with RMAF from 05 to 09 Aug 24 at Kuantan, Malaysia.
